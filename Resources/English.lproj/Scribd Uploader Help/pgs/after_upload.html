When your upload is finished

When your files have been uploaded and are ready, a sheet will appear with options on how you can proceed.

  • Edit on Website:  
    Clicking this button will take you to the Scribd.com website, where you can change information about your newly uploaded documents. You can change their titles, or make them more robust by setting categories, tags, and other metadata about them. This will help others find your documents more easily.
  • Close:  
    This button will dismiss the sheet and allow you to continue uploading other documents. If you upload more documents, this sheet will be redisplayed, and you will be able to edit information for all documents you’ve uploaded since you launched Scribd Uploader.

Scribd.com uses a title-cleaning tool to help guess the titles of your documents. For example, if you uploaded a document whose file name was “homeowner-checklist.xls,” Scribd.com will automatically title this document “Homeowner Checklist.” It might be a good idea to click the Edit on Website button to make sure the titles are all correct.
