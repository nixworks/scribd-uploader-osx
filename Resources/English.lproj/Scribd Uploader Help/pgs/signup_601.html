You must provide an email address and password to sign up for a Scribd account

This is an error that occurs when making a new Scribd.com account if you don’t provide all the required information. At a minimum, a new Scribd.com account must have a username, a valid email address, and a password. Read What’s a Scribd account? for more information, including email address privacy concerns.

Please make sure you have entered a valid email address, and that you have chosen a username and password for your new account, and try again.
