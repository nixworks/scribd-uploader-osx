How to share files using the Scribd Uploader

Scribd Uploader liberates your documents from your hard drive, getting them on the Internet for the world to see. In this tutorial, I’ll guide you through the steps you take to get your files onto the Scribd.com website where other can view them without special software from their own computers.

To upload your documents to Scribd.com:

  1. Add the files you want to upload to the file list.

    You can do this by either dragging and dropping the files into the file list, or by clicking the Add toolbar button and selecting them from the sheet that opens.

  2. Click the Upload button.

    A sheet will open where you can create a new Scribd.com account. What’s a Scribd account?

  3. Choose the “Get a Scribd account” tab.

    If you already have an account at Scribd.com, continue with I already have a Scribd account.

  4. Choose a username and password for yourself, and provide an email address.

    Signing up for an account with Scribd.com requires a valid email address. You must also choose a username and password that you can use to access the Scribd.com website. The Real name field is optional.

  5. Click the Sign Up button.

    If all goes well, the sheet should disappear and your files will begin uploading. The progress bars will fill as your files are uploaded. Once they are complete, a chime will sound and your files will be ready to view on the Scribd.com website.

    If an error occurs, continue with Solving problems.

Now that your documents are on Scribd.com, you can view them on the Web and share them with friends. Read How to view files you’ve uploaded to learn how to view your documents online.
