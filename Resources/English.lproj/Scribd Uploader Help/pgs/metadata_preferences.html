Metadata preference pane

The Metadata preference pane allows you to control how Scribd Uploader displays the Information drawer, which allows you to add and edit metadata before you upload your documents.

  • Automatically hide and show Information drawer:  
    If you check this box, the Information drawer will automatically appear when you have selected one or more files in the file list. When you clear your selection, the Information drawer disappears. If you wish to force the Information drawer to stay open or closed, uncheck this box.
