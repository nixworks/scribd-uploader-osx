Who can see my files?

By default, when you upload files to Scribd.com, anyone can view them. If you want to change this, read Uploading files that only I can see. Your document will appear in Scribd.com search results and in the browse page. If it’s popular, it may even be featured and appear on the home page or the hotlist!

Scribd.com also helps make your files more discoverable. Scribd employs Search Engine Optimization (SEO) techniques to make your documents more likely to appear on Google search results. This will help bring people to your document and get it noticed.

Files uploaded privately

If you uploaded your document privately, it will only be visible to you, and only when you log in to Scribd.com. It will not be shown on Google search results. A private document will never appear in the popular documents list or in the browse documents section of the Scribd.com website.

You can still let other people see your private documents if you wish; for more information, read Sharing private files with another trusted person.
