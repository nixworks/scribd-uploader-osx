Editing publisher information about your files

If you choose Show Publisher Panel from the Window menu, a panel will appear where you can change information about who authored or published your document. This information is useful for professional documents, to help keep track of different publications and editions. It also helps users understand who is responsible for distributing your document, and whether they can or cannot share it with their friends.

There are a number of different kinds of attribution and license information you can give about your document. All fields are optional. They are described here:

  • Author:  
    The name of the person or company that originally authored the work.
  • Publisher:  
    The name of the person or company that published this edition of the work.
  • Edition:  
    A name, number, or description of this edition of the work.
  • Date Published:  
    The date on which this edition of the work was first published. Click the Today button to automatically insert today’s date into this field.
  • License:  
    The license under which this work is distributed at Scribd. See Licenses for your document for a description of each license. Note that your selection here will only be displayed to the user; no website restrictions will be placed on how the user can use your uploaded document.
