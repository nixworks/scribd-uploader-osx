Uploading files that only you can see

By default, anyone can view files you upload to Scribd.com (see Who can see my files?). If you want only yourself or a trusted group of people to see your files, and no one else, follow the steps below.

To upload your files privately:

  1. Add the files you want to upload privately to the file list.

    See Adding files to share for more.

  2. Click the Information button on the toolbar, or select Show Information Panel from the Window menu.

    A drawer will slide out on the right side of the window with additional settings for your document.

  3. Select the document or documents you want to be private in your file list.

    You can hold down Command to select multiple documents, or Shift to select a range of documents.

  4. Check the “Upload as private” check box.

    Ensure that it is checked. If it is unchecked, your documents will be uploaded publicly (see below).

  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for any additional documents you want to be private.

  6. Click the Upload button.

Your files will be uploaded privately, and will only be viewable by you (see How to view files you’ve uploaded). If you want to share these files with trusted friends, continue to Sharing private files with another trusted person.

If you mistakenly upload your documents as public, you can still mark them as private later. Read Making documents private after uploading them for instructions.

If you intend to upload most of your files privately, you can tell Scribd Uploader to check the Upload as private checkbox by default. To do so, follow the instructions below.

To set all new files to private by default:

  1. Choose Preferences from the Scribd Uploader menu.

    The Preferences window will appear.

  2. Click the Uploading toolbar item.

  3. Enable the “Upload as private by default” checkbox.

Any files you add to Scribd Uploader from now on will be set to upload privately. If you want to publish a document, you must open the Information drawer and uncheck the Upload as private checkbox.
