Uploading files to an existing Scribd account

If you’ve already signed up for an account at Scribd.com, you can use Scribd Uploader to easily get your files onto your account. If you don’t have a Scribd.com account, read I don’t have a Scribd account. If you don’t know what a Scribd.com account is, read What’s a Scribd account?.

Before running these steps, make sure you’ve added some files to upload. Start with Adding files to share if you haven’t.

To upload your documents to an existing Scribd.com account:

  1. Make sure you are logged in as the correct user.

    At the top of the window, above the file list, you should see the text “Logged in as <your username>” or “Not logged in.” If you see someone else’s username, continue with Changing Scribd accounts.

  2. Click the Upload button.

    If you are asked for your Scribd account login and password, enter it now.

Your files will upload to the Scribd.com account you provided.
