How to keep your copy of Scribd Uploader up to date

Scribd Uploader has the ability to automatically check for updates to itself, and download and install those updates. This helps keep Scribd Uploader free of bugs and gets you new features when they are available.

You can either manually check for updates when you want, or use the auto-update feature to periodically and automatically check for updates. In no situation will an update be installed for you automatically; you will give permission for each install.

To have Scribd Uploader automatically check for updates:

  1. Select Preferences from the Scribd Uploader menu.

  2. Click the Automatic Updates toolbar button.

  3. Ensure the “Automatically check for updates” check box is checked. If it is not, click it.

To manually check for updates once:

  1. Choose Check for updates from the Scribd Uploader menu.
