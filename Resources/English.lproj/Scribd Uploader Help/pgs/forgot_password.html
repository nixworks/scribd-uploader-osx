I forgot my Scribd.com password

The Scribd.com website can help you change your password if you forget it. (We can’t tell you your old password, however, because we store them encrypted.) Follow these instructions to reset your password.

Changing a forgotten password:

  1. Open www.scribd.com in your web browser.

  2. Click the Log In link in the upper right-hand corner of the web page.

    If you instead see a Log Out link, click it, then click the Log In link.

  3. Click the “Trouble logging in?” link next to the Log In button.

  4. Enter the email address you used to sign up for your Scribd account.

    If you don’t remember your email address, or you no longer have access to it, you will need to contact Scribd.com for help. Read Who made this application? for more assistance.

  5. If you are asked for your Scribd.com account’s login, enter it in the new text field that appears.

    If you don’t remember your account username, you will need to contact Scribd.com for help. Read Who made this application? for more assistance.

  6. When you receive the email, click on the Reset Your Password button in the email to reset your password.

    The email will contain a button which, when clicked, will take you to a web page where you can change your password.

  7. Enter a new password for your Scribd.com account.

  8. Click Change Password.

The password for your Scribd.com account will now have been changed. You can use the new password the next time you upload files with Scribd Uploader.
