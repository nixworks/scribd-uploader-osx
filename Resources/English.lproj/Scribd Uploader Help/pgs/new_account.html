Uploading files to a new Scribd account

If you don’t yet have a Scribd.com account, you can upload your files to a new one. What’s a Scribd account? If you already have an account, read I already have a Scribd account.

Before running these steps, make sure you’ve added some files to upload. Start with Adding files to share if you haven’t.

To upload your documents to a new Scribd.com account:

  1. Make sure you are not logged in.

    At the top of the window, above the file list, you should see the text “Not logged in.” If you see some other text, continue with Changing Scribd accounts.

  2. Click the Upload button.

    A sheet will appear where you can enter information for your new Scribd.com account.

  3. Choose the “Get a Scribd account” tab.

  4. Choose a username and password for yourself, and provide an email address.

    Signing up for an account with Scribd.com requires a valid email address. You must also choose a username and password that you can use to access the Scribd.com website. The Real name field is optional.

    • Username:  
      This is a name for your Scribd.com account. It is an alias that your documents will be credited to, and you will also use it to log in to your account both in the application and on the website. If your name is John Smith, you might (for instance) have a username of “jsmith”, but it can be anything you want.
    • Password:  
      This is a secret password that only you know, to prevent other people from logging in to your Scribd.com account.
    • Email Address:  
      This is a valid email address which we will use to contact you if something important comes up, or if you forget your password. You can also use this to log in to your account instead of your username, if it’s easier to remember.
    • Real Name:  
      (optional) This is your real name, for credit on the documents you upload.

  5. Click the Sign Up button.

    If all goes well, the sheet should disappear and your files will begin uploading.

Your files will upload to the new Scribd.com account you created. To use this account, read How to view files you’ve uploaded.
