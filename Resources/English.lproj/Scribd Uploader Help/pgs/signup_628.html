There is already a Scribd user with that email address

This is an error that occurs when creating a Scribd.com account if you provide an email address that belongs to someone else already on the website. This is generally because of a few different scenarios.

You have already signed up for a Scribd.com account with that email: Log in to your existing Scribd.com account instead of creating a new one. If you forgot your password, read I forgot my Scribd.com password.

You are trying to sign up with an email address that belongs to someone else: You must use an email address that you own and have access to for your Scribd.com account.

Someone else has signed up for Scribd.com using your email address: You will need to reclaim your email address by proving that it’s yours. To do so, follow the steps below.

Reclaiming an email address:

  1. Open www.scribd.com in your web browser.

  2. Click the Sign Up link in the upper right-hand corner of the web page.

    A lightbox will appear where you can re-enter the account information.

  3. Enter the username, email address, and password you gave to Scribd Uploader.

  4. Click the Sign Up button.

    A second lightbox will appear where you can provide additional information for Scribd. You can skip this lightbox if you wish.

  5. After a short delay, check for new email at the address you provided. You should receive a new email from Scribd requesting that you verify your email address.

    The email may take some time to appear.

  6. Open the email and click the Verify Your Email button.

    The button will open a new page in your web browser indicating that your email address has been verified and claimed as yours.

Now that you have proven to Scribd.com that you are the owner of your email address, no one else will be able to make a new account with it.
