Uploading preference pane

The Uploading preference pane allows you to control how Scribd Uploader adds documents and uploads them to Scribd.com.

  • Upload as private by default:  
    If you check this box, new documents added to Scribd Uploader will be marked as private by default. If you want to upload a document publicly, you will have to uncheck the Upload as private checkbox in the Information drawer (see the related topics below).
