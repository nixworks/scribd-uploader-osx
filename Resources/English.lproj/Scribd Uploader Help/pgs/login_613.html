Your login and password didn’t match. Please double-check them and try again

This is an error that occurs when logging in to Scribd.com if you provide an incorrect username or password. When you signed up for a Scribd.com account, you were asked to create a username and password that, when used together, would allow you to access your documents on the website. Scribd Uploader needs this information in order to upload your documents to your Scribd.com account.

If you think you mistyped your username or password, re-enter them and try again. If you have forgotten your password, continue with I forgot my Scribd.com password.
