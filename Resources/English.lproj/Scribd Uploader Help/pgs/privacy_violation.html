Reporting a privacy concern or violation

Scribd.com takes your privacy very seriously. We take many efforts to protect your data, and we never intentionally violate your privacy. That said, it is seldom possible that an inadvertent privacy issue can occur. If you experience one, reporting it to us is the best way to ensure it never happens again.

To report a privacy violation, or if you have any privacy-related concerns or questions, send an email to privacy@scribd.com. Scribd pledges to respond within 30 days of receipt of the email.

Scribd’s privacy policy explains the steps we take to protect your data, and the rights and protections you have if your data is compromised. Learn more by visiting http://www.scribd.com/privacy in your web browser.
