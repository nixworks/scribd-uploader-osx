How to view files you’ve uploaded

Once you’ve uploaded your files, they become available on Scribd.com. To view any document you’ve just uploaded, simply click the next to the file. You can view all your documents together on Scribd.com by clicking the View All button that appears when all uploads have completed.

If you quit Scribd Uploader, the files you uploaded will be removed from the list. You can still go to Scribd.com to view these files by following the instructions below.

Manually viewing your documents on Scribd.com:

  1. Visit www.scribd.com in your web browser.

  2. Click the Log In link in the upper right hand corner of the web page.

    A lightbox will appear with forms where you will enter your login information.

  3. Enter the username and password for your Scribd.com account in the two leftmost fields of the lightbox.

    When you created your Scribd.com account, you specified a username (or email address) and a password to use when accessing Scribd.com. Enter that information now.

  4. Click the Login button.

    The lightbox will disappear and you will be logged in to the Scribd.com website, and taken to your home page.

  5. Click the My Docs link. (It’s the second link on the toolbar that runs along the top of the web page, below the Scribd logo.)

    You will see a list of the documents you uploaded.

  6. Click on a document title to view it.

Once you’re looking at your document, you can share it with the world. Above the viewer window are Share This and Send This buttons where you can share it with friends, and an Embed button where you can include your document in blogs.
